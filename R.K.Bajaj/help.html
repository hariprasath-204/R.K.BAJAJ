How to Book a Test Drive

1

Choose Your Bike

Browse our bike collection and select the model you want to test drive.

2

Fill the Form

Complete the test drive booking form with your personal details and preferred date/time.

3

Requirements

Ensure you have a valid driving license and are above 18 years of age.

4

Visit Showroom

Arrive at the scheduled time with your driving license and photo ID.

How to Book Your Bike

Step 1: Personal Details

Provide your basic personal information including name, contact details, and occupation.

Step 2: Address Details

Enter your current and permanent address information with complete details.

Step 3: Document Details

Provide government ID numbers including Aadhar, PAN, and Driving License.
